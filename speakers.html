Speakers

Francesco Franchi

Francesco Franchi is an award-winning Italian graphic designer who specializes in editorial design for news. Franchi has written several books on how to create eye-catching editorial layouts. He is now the creative director of IL, the magazine of Italian newspaper Il Sole 24 ORE.

Malaka Gharib

Malaka Gharib is an artist and journalist. Gharib is the deputy editor and digital strategist for NPR's blog Goats and Soda. Gharib is the author of I Was Their American Dream, a graphic memoir about her childhood as the daughter of Filipino and Egyptian immigrants.

Jeffrey Goldberg

Jeffrey Goldberg is a journalist and editor-in-chief at The Atlantic. He grew famous as a reporting covering foreign affairs, a time during which he won many awards.

Val Head

Val Head is an author and graphic designer whose focus lies in UX and UI animation. Head wrote the book Designing Interface Animation and writes the weekly UI Animation Newsletter.

Ezra Klein

Ezra Klein is a journalisst and blogger who co-founded Vox. Klein is currently the editor-at-large for Vox. He previously wrote blogs and columns for The Washington Post and was an associate editor at The American Prospect.

Roman Mars

Roman Mars is the host of 99% Invisible, a KALW radio show and podcast. Mars founded the podcast collective Radiotopia.

Manoush Zomorodi

Manoush Zomorodi is a journalist, podcast host and author. She hosted Note to Self on WNYC, which she quit in 2018. Zomorodi began her own media company called Stable Genius Productions with Jen Poyant, with whom she hosts the company's first podcast, ZigZag.

Ethan Zuckerman

Ethan Zuckerman is a blogger, internet activist and media scholar. Zuckerman currently works as the director of the MIT Center for Civic Media. He wrote the award-winning book Rewire: Digital Cosmopolitans in the Age of Connection.
